About PRAKALP

PRAKALP is a platform for engineering students to showcase innovative project models, working prototypes, and software simulations. Teams compete across various fields, presenting their concepts, designs, and technical knowledge.

This competition encourages creativity, interdisciplinary collaboration, and practical problem-solving.

Project Domains

Projects should fall under one of the following engineering domains:

  • CSE AND ALLIED SECTOR
  • ENTC/ELECTRICAL
  • MECHANICAL/CIVIL/ BIOTECHNOLOGY/CIVIL AND ENVIRONMENT/ROBOTICS AND AUTOMATION/AERONAUTICS

Rules & Guidelines

  • Open to UG, Diploma, and PG (MCA) students with valid college ID
  • Teams must have 2–6 members
  • Valid College/University ID Card is Mandatory
  • Send Abstract to Email Mentioning Subject as: kitcoekprakalp@gmail.com (Format: Name_College_Domain_Title)
  • Idea/Innovation Pitching Mode: Working Model/Software Simulation/Experimental Setup/ Poster/Prototype
  • Abstract: Max. 300 Words (Including but Not Limited to ldea/ Feasibility/Business/Scope/ Scalability, etc.)
  • Abstract Submission Deadline: 18th February 2026
  • Registration Fee: ₹ 200 рer Participant (Accommodation and Food for Outstation Participants included)
  • Prizes will be Awarded separately For each Domain
  • Incubation Support at KIT's IRF for Winners & interested Innovators/ Startups.
