About Abhivyakti

Abhivyakti is a national-level paper presentation competition that provides a platform for students to present innovative ideas, research work, and emerging technologies across all engineering disciplines.

Selected teams will get an opportunity to present their work at an international conference.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Paper must be in IEEE format with interdisciplinary content
  • Group Size : Min 1 participant - Max 4 participants
  • Paper submission deadline: 18th February 2026
  • Mail papers to: kitcoekabhivyakti@gmail.com Subject format: Name_College_Topic Name)
  • Registration fee:₹200 per participant (Non-Ref).
  • Publication: Selected papers, after peer review, will be published in an ISSN-registered Book Proceedings
  • Winners get an opportunity to present at an international conference
  • Prices will be awarded separately for each domain
