About Battle Blitzz

Battle Blitzz is a competitive Free Fire esports tournament conducted as part of PIONEER. Teams battle it out in intense squad-based matches to earn placement and kill points.

The finals will be conducted on LAN, while qualifying rounds will be held online.

Prize Distribution

Rules & Guidelines

  • Squad entry is compulsory (4 Members)
  • One or two Reserved members are allowed
  • There will be two Rounds of Battleblitz :
  • 1. Qualifying round (Online mode) on 15th February 2026 As per rules of qualification, Qualified teams are shortlisted for finale
  • 2.Final round will be offline mode at KITCoEK on 22nd February 2026
  • Management's decision will be final
  • Entry fee is non-refundable
