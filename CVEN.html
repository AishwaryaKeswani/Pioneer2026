About EN-Vision

EN-Vision is a project competition aimed at showcasing innovative and sustainable engineering solutions. Participants present ideas focused on environmental responsibility and real-world impact.

Event Topics

Rules & Guidelines

  • It should be Power Point Presentation or a prototype model
  • A team should consist of 1-4 members
  • Data comprised of text, figures, tables, charts, audio-visuals etc is allowed
  • Citing the reference is mandatory
  • Only shortlisted abstracts qualify for the final round
  • Presentation duration: under 7 minutes followed by Q&A
  • Participation certificate will be given after successful completion of presentation
  • The decision of the judges and event head shall be treated as final and binding to all
