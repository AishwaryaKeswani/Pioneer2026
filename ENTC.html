IdeaForge

IdeaForge is an idea presentation competition where participants propose innovative solutions to real-world problems in the form of a startup or product concept.

Ideas may be based on hardware, software, or integrated systems.

Prize Distribution

First Prize
₹5,000
Second Prize
₹3,000
Third Prize

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participation is open to individuals or teams
  • Maximum team size: 4 members
  • Ideas must be original and not copied from existing startups
  • Hardware, software, or hybrid solutions are allowed
  • Participants must follow the official presentation structure
  • Presentation time limits must be strictly followed
  • Use of clear visuals is allowed; heavy text is discouraged
  • Pre-existing work must be clearly disclosed
  • Internet access is allowed unless stated otherwise Plagiarism or misrepresentation will lead to immediate disqualification
  • Judges’ decisions will be final and binding
