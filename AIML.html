About Beyond Resume

Beyond Resume is a comprehensive competition designed to evaluate candidates beyond their academic credentials. This event focuses on real-world skills that matter in the professional world - aptitude, communication, and interpersonal abilities.

Participants will go through a rigorous selection process that mirrors actual recruitment procedures, providing valuable experience and insights into corporate hiring practices.

Event Format

The event will consist of four stages:

Stage 1: Aptitude Test

Aptitude test assessing reasoning and general engineering aptitude.

Stage 2: Technical Round

Technical Round to evaluate the programming skills..

Stage 3: Group Discussion

Shortlisted candidates participate in group discussions on contemporary topics. This stage assesses communication skills, leadership qualities, teamwork, and the ability to articulate ideas effectively.

Stage 4: One-on-One Interview

The final stage features personal interviews with industry experts. Candidates are evaluated on their technical knowledge, personality, confidence, and overall suitability for professional roles.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Top performers in each round will progress to the next Round.
  • The entire event will be conducted in English
  • No substitutions are allowed once registered
  • All participants must carry valid college ID cards
  • Professional attire is recommended for the interview round
  • Use of mobile phones or electronic devices during tests is strictly prohibited
  • Participants will be judged on skills, communication, and conduct throughout
  • Decisions of the judges and evaluators will be final and binding
  • Participants must report 15 minutes before their scheduled slot
