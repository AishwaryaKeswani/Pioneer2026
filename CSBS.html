About Tech-Negotia

The "Tech-negotia" is a fast-paced business and innovation challenge where teams select real-world problems, develop solutions, and competitively pitch their ideas.

It progresses from problem selection to an investor-style pitching round and concludes with a surprise final challenge testing quick thinking and decision- making, combining creativity, strategy, and investment simulation to identify the best team.

Event Format

Round 1: Problem Selection

Problems are shared 1 day before. Teams select one problem (first-come, first-served). Only 10 teams per problem. Teams submit a PPT or report.

Round 2: Investor Battle

Selected teams advance. Investors get virtual credits and invest in ideas they like. Top-invested teams qualify for the final.

Round 3: Final Surprise Round

Finalists receive an on-the-spot problem. Teams prepare quickly and present. Judges decide the winners. The judges' decision is final.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team should consist of 1-4 members.
  • Registration fee is ₹200 per team (non-refundable)
  • Presentations should be in PowerPoint or Word document format
  • Time limit for each presentation: 10 minutes, followed by 5 minutes Q&A
  • Judging criteria: innovation, feasibility, presentation quality, and negotiation skills
  • Decision of judges will be final and binding
  • Participants must carry their valid college ID cards
  • Code of conduct must be maintained throughout the event
