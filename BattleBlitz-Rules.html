Battle Blitz Rules & Regulations

Read carefully and follow all rules to ensure fair play and exciting competition!

Official Tournament Rules

Important

All participants must read and agree to follow these rules. Violation may result in disqualification.

It is a 4(+2) player team tournament. A 4 man-starter roster and up to 2 substitute.
Finals will be LAN and all the other qualifying round will be online.
All the matches will be Battle Royal.
All the matches will take place in Esports Mode.
Character skills are allowed.
Gun attributes are not allowed.
Emulators / Tablets are not allowed in any game mode organized. The player will be disqualified if found using any kind of emulator.
Any game modifying tools except 'GFX tool' is not allowed.
Players can play on android/ios phones only.
Only in-game voice chat should be used after the game is started till its completion.
Using hack or script of any kind is not allowed.
Any use of unfair means such as aimbot, trigger bot, ESP will be disqualified.
Should a team/player fail to join the room in time, their squad/they will be given 0 points for it.
The exploitation of bugs that hinders fair play will result in disqualification.
Organizers would not be held responsible for connectivity issues on the participant's side.
The entry fee would not be refunded under any circumstances.
Participants are requested to remain ready at least 15 minutes prior to the start of any match. Late entries would not be allowed.
Ties between two Teams during Scrims and Tournament points will be determined in the order of: (a) total times of winning the first placement across all the matches; (b) total accumulated placement points across all matches in the applicable matches and Tournament; (c) total accumulated finishes across all matches in the applicable Scrims and Tournament.
Team-up is not allowed.
Any use of Glitch/Bugs known/Unknown is not allowed.
Sit according to the slot List, if we found you sitting in any other team's slot then you'll be kicked out of the room.
If you doubt someone is hacking/cheating record your POV and send it to management.
Take the screenshot of your results, it maybe asked anytime.
Management's decision will be final regarding the time, rule, or any other objection.
Inviting random players in room will lead to dropping or cancelling the group match.
Don't Use an Emergency Pickup Kit Otherwise Your Points Will Not Get Count.
Take squad entry in rooms or you'll be responsible for misteaming of your players.
If any teams complaints against you and you failed to provide pov then you will get warning n 2 warnings will lead to disqualification.
Only 30+ id level is allowed otherwise record pov showing background app.
No allegation will be entertained without your pov + deathcam.
All 4 players have to record their pov with mic on. start your game and recording from apple store/play store.
If you are sitting on someone else slot and got a kick and then you are not able to join, in that case there is no rematch option. If someone is sitting on your slot, don't sit on any other slot. just wait on and contact management.

Fair Play Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a fair and competitive environment. All rules will be strictly enforced.
